It Start With Us

Author: Colleen Hoover

Genre: Romance novel, New adult fiction, Contemporary romance

Review: I haven't even finished this book yet I cant hold myself from saying that this is one of the best books I've ever read, period! When I started reading it, i thought okay its another romantic trope where the couple ends up together after all the fake dating stuff or some other trivial issues, atleast that's how most of the romance novels work, you know. But this book has been taking my breaths away ever since I started reading it! It's not in the world like what I had thought it'd be, it's a totally unique, heart touching, highly emotional, brilliant piece of work! One that explores a different side of love that not many authors try experimenting. The author has done a stellar job in putting the emotions into words in the most perfect way possible. I'm seriously in love with this book and can't wait to read it al out!
